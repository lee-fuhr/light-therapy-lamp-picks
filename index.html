How we choose our picks

We don’t run lab tests ourselves or conduct timed trials. Instead, we draw on the collective experience of thousands of owners, the expert measurements published by established reviewers (like wirecutter and consumer reports), and the long‑term failure patterns reported on retailers and forums. This approach reveals which lamps actually deliver on their brightness claims after a year of daily use and which ones develop flicker, dimming, or overheating. We also watch for design flaws that compromise therapy: a lamp that tips over easily, a glare that bothers your eyes, or a cord that’s too short. Our final picks are the models that consistently earn positive owner feedback across multiple seasons, remain available from reputable sellers, and represent a genuine difference in price or features, so you can match the lamp to your space and budget without guesswork.
